Holistic Jobs (1st Specialized Pharmaceutical Job Portal of Pakistan)

"Holistic Jobs" is a project of Holistic Solutions (Pvt.) Ltd. which is meant to facilitate employer and employees of the pharmaceutical industry who are otherwise waiting ONLY for reference based hiring which is obviously a big limitation of the hiring process.

It includes jobs from:
1) All hierarchical levels right from a Medical Representative to the level of HODs
2) All departments of Pharmaceutical Industry (Sales, Marketing, Production, QC/QA etc.)
3) All geographical areas from big cities to small towns
